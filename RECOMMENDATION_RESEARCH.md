# Recommendation research — July 17, 2026

This pass re-ranked the “worth doing” section for the exact Nov 12–29 trip. It triangulated:

1. official destination and attraction information for current operations;
2. recent independent travel blogs for routing and first-person tradeoffs;
3. high-volume TripAdvisor attraction reviews for repeated praise and complaints; and
4. recent traveler/local forum discussions for crowd, weather and pacing reality.

The public guide labels experiences **Essential**, **If clear**, **Quick hit** or **Choose one**. It also names attractions that should not consume protected trip time.

## Hong Kong

**Protect:** The Peak and Lugard Road, Central–Sheung Wan on foot, Star Ferry/harbourfront and Dragon’s Back when dry.

**Adjustment:** take bus 15 up when Peak Tram queues are long, then take the tram down. Treat Avenue of Stars as part of the harbour walk, not a destination. Do not stack paid skyline views.

Sources: [Hong Kong Tourism Board](https://www.discoverhongkong.com/eng/place-to-go/travel.guide-the-peak.html), [Star Ferry reviews](https://www.tripadvisor.com/Attraction_Review-g294217-d2228870-Reviews-Star_Ferry-Hong_Kong.html), [Time Out Hong Kong](https://www.timeout.com/hong-kong/things-to-do/11-tourist-attractions-hongkongers-never-go-to).

## Shenzhen

**Protect if the VOA is issued:** Huaqiangbei, Shenzhen Museum’s reform-era story and the Civic Center skyline from Lianhua Hill.

**Adjustment:** Ping An’s paid observation deck is weather-dependent. Skip Window of the World, Splendid China and distant Nanshan/Shekou stops on a border-constrained day trip.

Sources: [EyeShenzhen](https://www.eyeshenzhen.com/content/2025-06/04/content_31589717.htm), [Lianhuashan reviews](https://www.tripadvisor.com/Attraction_Review-g297415-d2051282-Reviews-Shenzhen_Lianhuashan_Park-Shenzhen_Guangdong.html), [Asia Undiscovered](https://www.asiaundiscovered.com/shenzhen-city/).

## Chongqing

**Protect:** riding Line 2 through Liziba, walking Shancheng Lane/Shibati, and seeing Hongya Cave illuminated from outside.

**Adjustment:** the Liziba street viewpoint is a quick photo, Hongya Cave’s retail interior is a skip, and the cableway needs a timed ticket or short queue. Use the ferry/riverside as the fallback.

Sources: [iChongqing](https://www.ichongqing.info/2026/04/30/beat-the-may-day-crowds-three-riverside-walks-that-unlock-chongqings-iconic-sights/), [Hongya Cave reviews](https://www.tripadvisor.com/Attraction_Review-g294213-d1814790-Reviews-Hongya_Cave-Chongqing.html), [TripZilla](https://www.tripzilla.com/honest-chongqing-review-singaporean/26814).

## Shanghai

**Protect:** the Bund at blue hour, an unhurried Former French Concession walk, Shanghai Museum East and the actual Yu Garden at opening.

**Adjustment:** use East Nanjing Road as a connector, skip the Bund sightseeing tunnel and avoid redundant observation decks. For the open Monday, Suzhou’s gardens are a stronger full-day change of scene than Zhujiajiao; West Bund is the better low-logistics rain choice.

Sources: [Meet in Shanghai](https://www.meet-in-shanghai.net/en/news/all-the-exhibitions-coming-to-shanghai-museum-in-2026-%E2%80%93-save-this-list-238693/), [Yu Garden reviews](https://www.tripadvisor.com/Attraction_Review-g308272-d324259-Reviews-Yu_Garden-Shanghai.html), [Notes from China](https://notesfromchina.com/blog/shanghai-3-day-itinerary/).

## Taipei

**Protect:** Longshan/Bopiliao/Dihua, a night market, a focused National Palace Museum visit and Elephant Mountain when visibility is good.

**Adjustment:** Beitou is the reliable drizzle-friendly finale. Jiufen is worthwhile early or late in decent visibility, but the rushed Yehliu–Shifen–Jiufen package is not. Raohe or Ningxia is easier to graze than Shilin.

Sources: [Taipei City](https://www.travel.taipei/en/attraction), [National Palace Museum reviews](https://www.tripadvisor.com/Attraction_Review-g13806879-d321216-Reviews-National_Palace_Museum-Shilin_Taipei.html), [Nick Kembel](https://www.nickkembel.com/taipei-itinerary-3-days/).

## Editorial rule

Popularity alone does not earn itinerary time. A recommendation stays only when it is distinctive to the destination, fits the route geographically, works in the expected November weather, and remains rewarding after accounting for queues and transit.
